*Straits Times Scraping*

http://api.queryly.com/json.aspx?
queryly_key=a7dbcffb18bb41eb
&query=facebook
&endindex=0
&batchsize=20
&facetedvalue=48
&facetedkey=pubDate

Queryly Key is always a7dbcffb18bb41eb, for Straits Times
The query is Facebook in this case
Taking end index / batch size yields the page number.
Batch size is the number of articles per page, max 100 min 1.
Timezone offset is 8 for 8 Hours ahead
FaceTedValue is the number of hours to search for (48 hours = 2 days back)
Leave FaceTedKey as pubDate
